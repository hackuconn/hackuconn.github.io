What is a Hackathon?

Simply put, a hackathon is an event that promotes a collaborative effort among teams to innovate and invent. As quoted from Major League Hacking, "Not to be confused with illegal and unauthorized programming, hacking in this context means quickly and intelligently creatng a real application that others can use."
