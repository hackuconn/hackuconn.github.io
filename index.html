What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event that promotes a collaborative effort among teams to innovate and invent. The HackUConn event aims to bring together motivated students, industry sponsors, and experienced mentors to encourage networking, creativity, and problem solving in a fast-paced team-oriented environment. Student teams will have the opportunity to pitch their ideas to a panel of industry experts at the conclusion of the event.
